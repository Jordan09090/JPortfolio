Business Morning Montage

Perth Commuter: A Day in the Life (ATAR Film Project)

Experience a glimpse into the daily routine of a Perth professional in this captivating morning montage.

This film, following Taj Rooling, portrays the dedication and focus required to navigate the daily commute and prepare for a busy workday. Witness the quiet determination as he transitions from the tranquility of his morning routine to the bustling city life.

Filmed entirely in Perth, WA, this project is the culmination of months of hard work by me, Jordan Bantleman, a Year 11 ATAR student. It serves as a testament to the commitment and perseverance required to achieve one's goals.

Settle in and join Taj on his journey, capturing the essence of the daily grind in Perth.
