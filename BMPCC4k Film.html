A Day At Wesley

Armed with the Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera 4K, I had the unique opportunity to document the vibrant journey of Year 7 students as they embarked on their educational path at Wesley College. This video, crafted entirely through the lens of the BMPCC 4K, not only captures the essence of orientation day but also stands as a testament to the camera's incredible ability to seize life's spontaneous moments with cinematic clarity. As I navigated through the throngs of eager faces and orientation activities, the camera became an extension of my creative vision, allowing me to tell a story of new beginnings with every frame.
